Sector 1, Greater Noida West

Sobha Rivana is a premier, upcoming luxury residential project featuring 8 high-rise towers (G+45) on a 12-acre development. Designed for modern, high-end living with low-density planning, this project offers a perfect blend of comfort, style, and exclusivity. The architecture is thoughtfully designed to maximize natural light and ventilation, providing residents with a serene and spacious living environment. Every detail, from the grand entrance lobby to the landscaped gardens, reflects the meticulous craftsmanship that SOBHA is renowned for.
